## 1. What this is

A proposal to recognize "Life-Needs Concierge" as a **named vertical inside the existing Orchestration Strategy** — not a new strategy, not a new entity, not a pivot away from ACAT.

**Scope:** The vertical exists to coordinate, verify, document, and route client needs across licensed or credentialed specialists. It does **not** provide medical, legal, or benefits advice itself — it is administrative and documentary coordination, not practice.

**Primary beachhead:** Healthcare navigation, care coordination, records translation, and regulated multi-system case ownership. This is the clearest match to the resume evidence (hospital QA, EMR conversion, regulatory auditing, lab operations, service recovery under pressure) and is the only domain active in Phase 1.

**Adjacent lanes (not active in Phase 1):** Elder-care coordination and government-benefits navigation, provided the work stays administrative, documentary, and referral-based. Legal paperwork is in scope only as document routing and process support — never legal counsel or representation.

The Orchestration Strategy already defines the structural model this vertical would use:

| Role | Function (per Orchestration Strategy V2) |
|---|---|
| Client | Defines the need, accepts the deliverable |
| Orchestrator (HumanAIOS) | Vets, briefs, monitors, verifies, owns the outcome |
| Specialist | Delivers licensed/credentialed service within their domain, on spec |

The RentAHuman Integration Plan's ACAT-dimension fit-scoring concept is noted as a future matching mechanism but is **explicitly out of scope** for this proposal and for Phase 1 (see Section 4). The worker pool for this vertical — credentialed healthcare-navigation specialists — is a separate sourcing problem from RentAHuman's gig-task pool and is not addressed here.

## 2. Why this is being proposed now

Resume review (this session) surfaced a structural fact: the existing operations background (regulatory quality auditing, healthcare compliance, multi-system case ownership, EMR migration data integrity) is a stronger match to the *orchestrator* role in a life-needs vertical than to most other monetization paths explored to date. This proposal exists to test whether that match is worth ratifying as a direction, not to commit resources to building it.

## 3. P5 check

P5: output must generate research data OR test a hypothesis OR generate revenue before proceeding.

- **Research data:** Possible secondary benefit — if built, ACAT-style calibration scoring could be tested against a new worker population (credentialed healthcare-navigation specialists vs. gig workers), extending H-SELF-01/F-50-adjacent questions about instrument portability across worker types. Not the primary justification, and not active in Phase 1.
- **Hypothesis tested:** Candidate hypothesis — *"Operations background in regulated, multi-system case ownership predicts concierge-orchestrator fit better than AI-research framing alone."* This is testable cheaply (see Phase 1 below) without building infrastructure.
- **Revenue:** Direct path — concierge/orchestration services are a more legible, immediately billable offering than ACAT research outputs. This is the strongest P5 justification.

**P5 disposition:** PASSES on revenue grounds, contingent on Phase 1 being cheap enough to fit inside the charter window without displacing charter-critical work (Longview RFPs, H-FORMAT-01, HA-000).

## 4. Scope against charter close (17 days remaining)

This proposal is scoped to **not** require any build, hire, or external commitment before July 16. Two phases:

**Phase 0 — Z2 decision only (this proposal).** Night ratifies, modifies, or kills the *framing*. No resources committed. Zero conflict with Longview deadlines (July 2, July 10).

**Phase 1 — if ratified, post-charter.** Positioning-only test: rewrite resume and LinkedIn materials to emphasize orchestrator-fit capabilities — case ownership, regulated-system translation, audit discipline, and crisis response — anchored to the healthcare-navigation beachhead. No platform build, no hiring, no new entity, and no external delivery commitment are included in Phase 1. Cost: a few hours of Z1 drafting time. This phase explicitly does NOT touch HA-000, Longview submissions, H-FORMAT-01, or any Z3 terminal item.

**Explicit exclusions (any phase, until separately ratified):**
- Direct clinical care, legal practice, or benefits adjudication
- Specialist credentialing or vetting infrastructure
- Any orchestration engine or matching infrastructure (including ACAT-fit-scoring applied to this vertical)
- Recruiting specialists, standing up a new brand or LLC structure, any Supabase/GitHub infrastructure work
- Any expansion into the adjacent lanes (elder-care, government-benefits) — these remain named-but-inactive until their own Z2 ratification
