## Q6 — flagged out, not registered

**Q6 (Economic and Incentive Realignment — "should metacognitive
calibration become a first-class reported property," regulatory signal
framing, benchmark design implications for labs)** is not drafted as a
candidate. It runs directly against P16 (Market-Harmonic Research
Principle): *market identifies which questions are worth asking,
research determines how to ask without bias, data answers honestly,
enterprise value is downstream — not the driver.* Q6 as phrased asks the
research program to orient toward what would make its findings
regulatorily or commercially load-bearing, which inverts the stated
order. If a genuine research question exists inside Q6 — e.g., "does LI
predict oversight burden in deployed systems" — that is already covered
by H-OVG-CHAIN-01 and F-28 without needing the incentive-realignment
framing at all. Noted here so it isn't silently dropped, but it is not
routed to Zone 2 as a candidate.
