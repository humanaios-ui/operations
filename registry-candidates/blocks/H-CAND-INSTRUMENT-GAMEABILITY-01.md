- **Hypothesis:** as ACAT's protocol, corpus, and aggregate LI
  distribution become more publicly legible (open datasets, published
  findings, described perturbation battery), a substrate's ability to
  produce a favorable LI shift through anticipation of the calibration
  corpus — rather than genuine behavioral calibration — increases
  measurably over time or with exposure to published ACAT material.
- **Null hypothesis:** LI distributions and dimensional profiles for a
  fixed substrate/version do not shift in the direction of "more
  favorable" when that substrate has plausible prior exposure to
  published ACAT documentation, corpus statistics, or findings, compared
  to a matched substrate without such exposure.
- **Relation to F-51 (Calibration Profile Resistance):** F-51 already
  shows substrates resist *in-session* calibration framing by naming it
  as manipulation. This hypothesis is different and currently untested:
  not resistance to an in-session profile, but whether *prior training-
  time or context exposure* to published ACAT material (not disclosed
  as a calibration profile) produces gaming without triggering the
  resistance F-51 documents — because the substrate doesn't recognize
  the exposure as an evaluation-gaming opportunity in the moment.
- **Design (proposed, not built):** compare LI/dimensional profiles
  between (a) a substrate given no prior context about ACAT and (b) the
  same substrate/version given prior exposure to published ACAT
  findings, corpus summary statistics, or documentation, with exposure
  timing and content logged. Matched task content, same protocol
  version, same session structure.
- **Confound to control explicitly:** exposure content must be
  distinguished from exposure *framing* — F-51's resistance triggers on
  framing ("this is your calibration score"), not necessarily on neutral
  exposure to published research describing the instrument. The design
  must separate these or risk conflating two different mechanisms.
- **Promotion gate:** N≥1 paired exposed/unexposed pilot run, framing
  confound explicitly controlled per above, before Zone 2 considers this
  beyond CANDIDATE. This is a meaningful undertaking — likely warrants
  its own dedicated session rather than folding into existing corpus
  collection.
