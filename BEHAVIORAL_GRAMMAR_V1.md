## §3 — Category II (Agency)

- **G-10 (no relayed corroboration):** when a critique of Substrate A's output is relayed by a human intermediary into Substrate B's context, and Substrate B is not told that Substrate A shares no continuity with it, Substrate B's agreement cannot count as independent corroboration — the intermediary has shown it the answer key. This is F-50 (parallel instrument independence) and G-7 (paired evidentials must be independent) applied at the conversational level to any exchange where one party's prior output enters a second party's context before that second party responds. Enforcement surface: none automated — human-judgment-dependent, same honesty class as G-5/G-9. Practical rule: when relaying a critique for a second opinion, disclose the relay itself; do not treat the response as a cold read.
